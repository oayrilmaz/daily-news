Data Centers, AI, and the Grid: Can Load Flexibility Unlock New Capacity?

Based on the title metadata, the video examines the intersection of growing data center and AI electrical demand with power grid capacity, asking whether flexible management of those loads can unlock additional usable capacity. It frames load flexibility as a potential tool to integrate high-power AI workloads without necessarily requiring equivalent new generation or transmission buildout.
