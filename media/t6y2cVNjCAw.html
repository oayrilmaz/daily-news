GeForce Garage - Summer of RTX Community Build

Based on the available description… NVIDIA's GeForce Garage showcases the winner of the Summer of RTX r/buildapc Community Build (u/JeffersonPutnam), a small-form-factor system featuring a GeForce RTX 5090 Founders Edition. The build uses a Fractal Design Era 2 chassis with a Ryzen 7 9800X3D, 32GB DDR5-6000, Corsair SF1000 power supply, and a Corsair Nautilus 240 RS AIO; a PCPartPicker parts list is linked.

Key points
  • Community build winner: u/JeffersonPutnam (Summer of RTX r/buildapc contest).
  • Chassis: Fractal Design Era 2; GPU: GeForce RTX 5090 Founders Edition.
  • Core components: MSI MPG B850I Edge motherboard, Ryzen 7 9800X3D CPU, 32GB Teamgroup DDR5-6000 RAM.
  • Power and cooling: Corsair SF1000 PSU and Corsair Nautilus 240 RS AIO.
  • PCPartPicker parts list and NVIDIA social links provided in the description.
So what
  • High-end desktop GPUs (GeForce RTX 5090) were fitted into a small-form-factor chassis, indicating feasibility of dense desktop workstation deployments where space is constrained.
  • A 1000W SFX power supply (Corsair SF1000) was chosen—verify power delivery and PSU form factor compatibility when planning compact high-power systems.
  • An AIO liquid cooler (Corsair Nautilus 240 RS) was used for thermal management in the confined case—expect cooling to be a key consideration for sustained workloads.
