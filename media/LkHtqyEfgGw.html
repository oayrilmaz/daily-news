So what

  • Utilities and IPPs need tailored procurement, rate designs and demand-response programs to manage concentrated AI demand spikes.
  • EPCs and data-center owners should prioritize energy-efficient design, power-path optimization and integration of storage/renewables to reduce bill impact.

  • Energy buyers and facility operators should quantify load profiles of AI workloads to negotiate contracts and deploy flexibility resources.
