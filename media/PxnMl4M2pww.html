Why data is critical to sustainable supply chains

Based on the available description… David Linich, Sustainability Principal at PwC US, explains that reliable, high-quality data enables organizations to scenario plan, protect margins, and deliver sustainable, high-quality products at the right place and time. With conditions changing week by week, he emphasizes that data-driven decision-making is critical to building resilient, modern supply chains.

Watch on YouTube
Key points
  • Reliable, high-quality data supports scenario planning for supply-chain disruptions.
  • Data helps organizations protect margins amid shifting tariffs and cost pressures.
  • Accurate data enables delivery of sustainable, high-quality products to the right place and time.
  • Supply-chain conditions can change week by week, increasing the need for timely data.
  • Data-driven decision-making is presented as essential for resilient, modern supply chains.
So what
  • Use reliable, high-quality supply-chain data to run scenario planning for capacity and risk.
  • Leverage data to protect margins by informing sourcing and logistics decisions.
  • Ensure procurement and delivery decisions are informed by data to meet sustainability goals and quality targets.
  • Monitor week-by-week supply conditions with data to adapt operations quickly.
  • Prioritize data-driven decision processes to strengthen overall supply-chain resilience.
