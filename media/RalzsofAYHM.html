29-year-old spent $20,000 to open a store in New York — now it brings in $1.6 million a year

Based on the available description, Abby Price started Abbode as a Parsons graduate student, selling dried floral arrangements to New Yorkers via Facebook in 2019. By March 2022 she had a Nolita storefront selling bouquets and home decor and used the shop’s cashflow to buy a $15,000 embroidery machine she purchased “on a total whim,” after a key item sat covered in dust in the basement for a year.
