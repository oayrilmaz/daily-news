The former crown prince of Iran on its path to democracy

Based on the available description, the exiled son of Iran’s former shah, Reza Pahlavi, told The Economist he would act as a neutral arbiter during a transition, oversee free elections and a referendum, and then transfer power to an elected parliament. The remarks come in an interview featured on The Economist's Insider show.
