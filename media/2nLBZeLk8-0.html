AI w cenach transferowych – jak wykorzystać jej potencjał? | Monika Palmowska, Jakub Roszkiewicz

Based on the available description… AI is already reshaping transfer pricing workstreams—supporting monitoring of profitability, comparative analyses and automation of documentation. KPMG experts note AI is likely to strengthen advisors rather than replace them, while flagging risks (data security, RODO, hallucinations) and practical implementation steps like process cleanup, ERP integration and using secure environments such as Digital Gateway.

Watch on YouTube
Key points
  • AI use cases: rentability monitoring, benchmark analyses, and TP documentation automation.
  • AI augments tax advisors' work but does not replace professional judgement.
  • Key risks include data protection, security and AI hallucinations; compliance with RODO is highlighted.
  • Digital Gateway presented as a secure environment for AI work.
  • Implementation priorities: organise processes, integrate AI with ERP, and build intelligent benchmarks.
So what
  • Provide or enable secure, compliant environments (e.g., Digital Gateway) for AI workloads to protect sensitive tax data.
  • Ensure strict data governance and RODO-compliant handling as a precondition for AI in transfer pricing.
  • Support integration capabilities with ERP and benchmarking tools to enable AI-driven workflows and analyses.
  • Prioritise process standardisation and clear data pipelines before AI rollout to reduce operational risk.
  • Plan for upskilling and governance processes to manage AI outputs and mitigate hallucination risks.
