AI w cenach transferowych – jak wykorzystać jej potencjał? | Monika Palmowska, Jakub Roszkiewicz

Based on the available description… The podcast discusses how AI is changing transfer pricing—enabling monitoring of profitability, comparative analyses and automation of documentation—while exploring practical implementation, integration with ERP, and future use cases. KPMG experts Monika Palmowska and Jakub Roszkiewicz emphasize that AI will strengthen rather than replace tax advisors and highlight risks (data, security, RODO, hallucinations) and initial steps for adoption.

Watch on YouTube
Key points
  • AI applications in transfer pricing: profitability monitoring, intelligent benchmarks and automated documentation
  • AI as a tool to augment, not replace, tax advisors
  • Implementation topics: integration with ERP, persona-driven approaches and first steps to deploy AI
  • Risks and compliance: data security, GDPR/RODO concerns and model hallucinations
  • Secure environments (Digital Gateway) and process organization as foundations for AI rollout
So what
  • Consider AI to monitor profitability and benchmarking of intercompany services to improve pricing visibility
  • Automating transfer pricing documentation can reduce manual effort and speed compliance workflows
  • Ensure secure, compliant environments (e.g., Digital Gateway) and address GDPR/data-security requirements before deployment
  • Plan ERP integration for intelligent benchmarks and analytics to get actionable insights from AI
  • Begin with process cleanup and staff upskilling as foundational steps for successful AI adoption
