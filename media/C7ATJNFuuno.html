AI-Powered Product Catalog Revolution: From Photos to Rich Listings in Minutes

Based on the available description, AWS demonstrates a generative AI solution that automates fast-fashion cataloging by creating product attributes, SEO-optimized descriptions, and virtual try-on images. The solution uses Amazon Bedrock (Nova Pro, Nova Canvas) and Claude Sonnet 3.5 alongside Amazon Rekognition to cut time-to-market from weeks to minutes, reduce cataloging labor, and improve customer engagement for retail and CPG B2B customers.

Watch on YouTube
Key points
  • Automates product attributes, descriptions, and virtual try-on image generation for fast-fashion catalogs.
  • Built on Amazon Bedrock (Nova Pro, Nova Canvas) and Claude Sonnet 3.5, with Amazon Rekognition for image processing.
  • Reduces time-to-market from weeks to minutes and produces SEO-optimized content.
  • Supports brand-specific voice plus customization for body types, poses, and emotions in virtual try-on.
  • Aims to cut operational cataloging costs and free resources for strategic priorities, helping retailers respond faster to trends.
So what
  • Solution relies on Amazon Bedrock and Amazon Rekognition — compatibility and support for those services are central for customer deployments.
  • Faster, high-volume content generation (minutes vs weeks) implies demand for scalable, AI-ready compute and storage capacity.
  • Image-heavy virtual try-on and customized rendering workflows will increase requirements for GPU/accelerated processing and storage throughput.
  • Opportunities for data center providers to offer managed AI infrastructure or integration services that support retail/CPG adoption of these workflows.
