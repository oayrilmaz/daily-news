Grid Interconnection Delays Impact Energy Storage Project Deployment

High confidence — Industry reports and interconnection queue data confirm growing delays for storage assets.

TL;DR

Extended interconnection timelines are increasingly affecting the deployment schedules of utility-scale energy storage projects.

Why it matters

  • Slows grid flexibility improvements needed for renewable integration.
  • Increases project financing risks and costs.
  • Complicates utility resource planning and reliability strategies.

Key points

  • Interconnection processes often lack clear criteria for storage-specific impacts.
  • Regulatory bodies are reviewing procedures to better accommodate storage.

  • Some regions prioritize storage projects to address grid needs.
  • Delays may discourage investment in storage capacity expansion.
