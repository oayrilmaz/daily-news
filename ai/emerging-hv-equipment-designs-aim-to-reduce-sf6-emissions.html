Manufacturers are developing alternative insulation technologies to replace SF6 gas in high-voltage equipment due to environmental concerns.

  • Reducing SF6 emissions aligns with corporate sustainability goals.

Key points

  • Performance and reliability testing is ongoing to meet grid requirements.

  • Regulations in Europe and North America are tightening SF6 use limits.
  • Manufacturers invest in R&D to balance environmental impact and technical performance.
  • Market acceptance depends on cost parity and proven reliability.
  • Standards bodies are updating guidelines to incorporate alternatives.
