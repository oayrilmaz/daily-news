Data Center Energy Consumption Trends in 2024

High confidence — Consistent industry reports and energy usage data from major operators support this trend.

TL;DR

Data centers continue to increase energy consumption driven by AI workloads, despite efficiency improvements in hardware and cooling technologies.

Why it matters

  • Rising energy demand impacts grid load profiles and peak demand management.
  • Increased operational costs for data center operators due to electricity price volatility.
  • Pressure on renewable integration and carbon reduction targets.

Key points

  • AI and machine learning workloads are primary drivers of increased compute demand.
  • Adoption of liquid cooling and advanced HVAC systems is improving energy efficiency.
  • Shift towards on-site renewable generation is gaining momentum.
  • Data centers are increasingly located near renewable energy sources to optimize power supply.
  • Energy procurement strategies are evolving to include long-term power purchase agreements (PPAs).
