TL;DR

FACTS devices are increasingly deployed to improve grid flexibility and stability with high renewable energy penetration.

Why it matters

  • Enables dynamic voltage and power flow control.
  • Mitigates congestion and enhances transmission capacity.
  • Supports integration of variable renewable generation.

Key points

  • FACTS include devices like STATCOMs, SVCs, and UPFCs.

  • Deployment is growing in regions with stressed transmission networks.
  • FACTS reduce need for costly infrastructure upgrades.
  • Integration with HVDC and energy storage is advancing.
