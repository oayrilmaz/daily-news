GIS Technology Adoption Expands in Emerging Markets

Medium confidence — Market reports indicate growth, but deployment data remains uneven.

TL;DR

Gas-insulated switchgear (GIS) is gaining traction in emerging markets due to urbanization and the need for reliable compact substations.

Why it matters

  • Supports grid expansion in space-constrained urban areas.
  • Improves reliability and reduces maintenance compared to AIS.
  • Facilitates integration of distributed energy resources.

Key points

  • Emerging markets prioritize GIS for dense metropolitan grids.
  • Cost remains a barrier but is offset by land savings.
  • Local manufacturing and partnerships are increasing.
  • Training and technical expertise development is ongoing.
  • Environmental regulations influence GIS adoption.
  • Modular designs improve scalability and installation speed.
