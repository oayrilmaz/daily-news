Emerging Digital Twins Enhance HV Equipment Asset Management

Medium confidence — Several pilot projects demonstrate benefits, but industry-wide adoption is nascent.

TL;DR

Digital twin technology is increasingly applied to high voltage equipment, enabling predictive maintenance and operational optimization.

Why it matters

  • Improves equipment reliability and reduces unplanned outages.
  • Optimizes maintenance schedules, lowering operational costs.
  • Supports integration of distributed energy resources.

Key points

  • Digital twins create virtual replicas of physical assets using sensor data.
  • They enable simulation of equipment behavior under various conditions.
  • Integration with SCADA and asset management systems is advancing.
  • Data analytics identify early signs of equipment degradation.
  • Cybersecurity and data quality are critical challenges.
  • Collaboration between utilities and technology providers is growing.
  • Standardization efforts are underway to facilitate interoperability.
