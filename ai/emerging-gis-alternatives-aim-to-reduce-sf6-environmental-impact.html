Emerging GIS Alternatives Aim to Reduce SF6 Environmental Impact

Medium confidence — Laboratory and pilot scale tests show promise, but commercial adoption remains limited.

TL;DR

Research into alternative insulating gases and vacuum technologies is progressing to replace SF6 in gas-insulated switchgear (GIS) and reduce greenhouse gas emissions.

Why it matters

  • SF6 has a high global warming potential.
  • Regulatory pressure is increasing to limit SF6 emissions.
  • Alternative technologies could future-proof GIS installations.

Key points

  • Alternatives include fluoronitrile-based gases and vacuum interrupters.
  • Performance and insulation properties vary among alternatives.
  • Cost and compatibility with existing equipment are key considerations.
  • Pilot projects are underway in Europe and Asia.
  • Standards development is ongoing for new insulating media.
  • Potential to reduce lifecycle environmental footprint.
  • Industry collaboration is critical for technology validation.
