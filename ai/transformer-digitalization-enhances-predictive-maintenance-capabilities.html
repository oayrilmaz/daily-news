Transformer Digitalization Enhances Predictive Maintenance Capabilities

High confidence — Multiple utilities have deployed digital transformer monitoring with documented benefits.

Integration of sensors and IoT in transformers is enabling condition-based monitoring and predictive maintenance to reduce outages.
