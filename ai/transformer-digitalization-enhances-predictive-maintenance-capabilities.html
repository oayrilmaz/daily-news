Transformer Digitalization Enhances Predictive Maintenance Capabilities

High confidence — Several utilities have deployed digital transformer monitoring systems with documented benefits.

TL;DR

Integration of sensors and IoT technologies in transformers enables real-time monitoring and predictive maintenance, reducing downtime and operational costs.

Why it matters

  • Improves asset management and extends transformer life.
  • Reduces unplanned outages and maintenance expenses.
  • Supports data-driven grid reliability strategies.

Key points

  • Sensors monitor temperature, load, oil quality, and partial discharge.
  • Data analytics identify early signs of degradation or faults.
  • Enables condition-based rather than time-based maintenance.
  • Integration with utility asset management systems is increasing.
  • Cybersecurity and data management are critical considerations.

  • Standards development for digital transformer monitoring.

Known unknowns
