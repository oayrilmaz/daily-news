TL;DR

HVDC technology adoption is accelerating to enable efficient long-distance and cross-border power transfer, especially for renewables.

Why it matters

  • HVDC reduces transmission losses over long distances.
  • Facilitates integration of remote renewable generation.
  • Supports grid interconnections enhancing energy security.

Key points

  • HVDC lines are preferred for underwater and underground transmission.
