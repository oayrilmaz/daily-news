Transformer Digital Twins Enhance Asset Management

Medium confidence — Early adoption by select utilities with promising but limited data.

TL;DR

Digital twin technology is being adopted for transformers to simulate performance, predict failures, and optimize maintenance.

Why it matters

  • Improves reliability and reduces unplanned outages.
  • Enables data-driven maintenance scheduling and cost savings.
  • Facilitates integration with smart grid and IoT systems.

Key points

  • Digital twins combine real-time sensor data with simulation models.
  • Allows scenario analysis for stress conditions and aging effects.
  • Integration with asset management platforms supports decision-making.
  • Pilot projects report improved fault detection accuracy.
  • Challenges include data integration and model validation.
  • Vendor solutions are emerging with cloud-based platforms.
  • Potential to extend to other HV equipment beyond transformers.
