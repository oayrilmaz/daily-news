Transformer Innovation Focuses on Efficiency and Digital Monitoring

Medium confidence — Emerging product launches and pilot projects reported but large-scale adoption pending.

Transformer manufacturers are integrating advanced materials and digital sensors to improve efficiency and enable predictive maintenance.

Why it matters

Key points

  • Use of amorphous steel cores reduces no-load losses significantly.
  • Digital sensors monitor temperature, load, and partial discharge in real-time.
