TL;DR

Transformer manufacturers and utilities are increasing focus on recycling and circular economy practices to reduce environmental impact and raw material dependency.

Why it matters

  • Reduces demand for critical raw materials and waste generation.
  • Lowers lifecycle environmental footprint of power equipment.
  • Supports regulatory compliance and sustainability goals.

Key points

  • Recycling of copper, steel, and insulating oils is expanding.
  • Development of standards for transformer end-of-life management.
  • Design for disassembly gaining attention to facilitate recycling.

  • Economic incentives for recycling remain limited.
  • Research ongoing into reuse of transformer components.
  • Potential to reduce supply chain risks for raw materials.
