Recycling of rare earth elements is emerging as a strategic approach to reduce supply risks and environmental impact in high-voltage equipment manufacturing.

  • Supports circular economy principles in the energy sector.

  • Recycling processes focus on magnets from end-of-life equipment.

  • Policy incentives may accelerate recycling adoption.
