HVDC Projects Gain Momentum for Long-Distance Renewable Power Transmission

High confidence — Numerous large-scale HVDC projects are under construction or planning globally with confirmed financing.

TL;DR

High Voltage Direct Current (HVDC) technology adoption is accelerating to enable efficient long-distance transmission of renewable energy.

Why it matters

  • Enables integration of remote renewable resources into main grids.
  • Reduces transmission losses compared to AC lines.
  • Supports interconnection between asynchronous grids.

Key points

  • HVDC lines can transmit power over thousands of kilometers with minimal losses.
  • Multi-terminal HVDC systems are emerging for complex grid interconnections.
  • Voltage Source Converter (VSC) technology enhances controllability.
  • Costs are declining with technological improvements and scale.
  • Projects in Europe, Asia, and North America are advancing rapidly.
  • Regulatory frameworks are adapting to accommodate HVDC integration.
