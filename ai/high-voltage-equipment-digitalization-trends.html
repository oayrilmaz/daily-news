High Voltage Equipment Digitalization Trends

Medium confidence — Pilot projects and early deployments show promising results, but broad adoption is ongoing.

TL;DR

Digital technologies are increasingly integrated into high voltage equipment to enhance monitoring, diagnostics, and operational efficiency.

Why it matters

  • Improves asset management and reduces unplanned outages.
  • Enables predictive maintenance and extends equipment life.
  • Supports grid operators in managing complex networks.

Key points

  • Sensors and IoT devices collect real-time equipment data.
  • Advanced analytics identify early signs of failure.
  • Digital twins simulate equipment behavior under various conditions.
  • Cybersecurity measures are critical for connected equipment.
  • Integration with SCADA and energy management systems is expanding.
  • Manufacturers offer retrofit kits for legacy equipment.
  • Cost-benefit analyses support investment decisions.

  • Cybersecurity incidents and mitigation strategies.
