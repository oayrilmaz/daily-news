Advanced Monitoring Enhances HV Equipment Reliability

Medium confidence — Pilot projects demonstrate benefits, but widespread adoption is still emerging.

Deployment of advanced sensors and AI analytics is improving the reliability and predictive maintenance of high voltage equipment.

Why it matters
