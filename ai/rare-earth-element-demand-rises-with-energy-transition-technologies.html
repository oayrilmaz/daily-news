Demand for rare earth elements (REEs) is increasing sharply due to their critical role in permanent magnets for wind turbines and electric vehicle motors.

Why it matters

  • Supply constraints could impact renewable energy and EV manufacturing.
  • Geopolitical concentration of REE production poses supply risks.
  • Stimulates investment in recycling and alternative material research.

  • China dominates global REE production and processing capacity.

  • Substitution research aims to reduce reliance on critical REEs.
