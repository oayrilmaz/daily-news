Advancements in Transformer Digital Monitoring and Predictive Maintenance

Medium confidence — Pilot projects and early deployments demonstrate benefits, but widespread adoption is ongoing.

Digital sensors and AI analytics are increasingly integrated into transformers to enable real-time condition monitoring and predictive maintenance.
