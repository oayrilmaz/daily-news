Transformer Digitalization Enables Predictive Maintenance

Medium confidence — Pilot programs show promising results, but widespread deployment is ongoing.

TL;DR

Integration of sensors and IoT technologies in transformers allows utilities to shift from reactive to predictive maintenance strategies.

Why it matters

  • Reduces unplanned outages and improves asset reliability.
  • Optimizes maintenance schedules and lowers operational costs.
  • Provides data for better asset management and investment planning.

Key points

  • Sensors monitor temperature, moisture, and partial discharge.
  • Data analytics identify early signs of equipment degradation.
  • Cloud platforms enable centralized monitoring across fleets.
  • Integration challenges exist with legacy equipment.
  • Cybersecurity considerations are critical for data integrity.
  • Training and workforce adaptation required for new technologies.
  • Expected to extend transformer service life and reduce failures.

  • Advances in AI for predictive analytics.
