TL;DR

Utilities are increasingly deploying synchronous condensers to improve grid inertia and voltage regulation amid rising renewable integration.

Why it matters

  • Supports grid stability as renewable penetration increases.
  • Reduces reliance on fossil-fuel-based spinning reserves.
  • Enables smoother integration of variable energy sources.

Key points

  • Synchronous condensers provide reactive power support and inertia.
  • Deployment is growing in regions with high wind and solar capacity.
  • Technological improvements have reduced operational costs.
  • They can be retrofitted from existing generators or built new.
  • Grid codes are evolving to recognize their value explicitly.
  • They help mitigate frequency fluctuations and voltage dips.
