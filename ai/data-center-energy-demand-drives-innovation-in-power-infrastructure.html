Data Center Energy Demand Drives Innovation in Power Infrastructure

Medium confidence — Industry reports indicate growth trends, but specific infrastructure upgrades vary by region.

TL;DR

Rapid growth in data center capacity is prompting utilities to upgrade power delivery and incorporate advanced grid technologies.

Why it matters

  • Data centers represent a significant and growing load on power grids.
  • Require highly reliable and low-latency power supply.
  • Drive adoption of energy-efficient and resilient grid technologies.

Key points

  • Data centers often co-locate with renewable generation and storage.
  • Use of synchronous condensers and advanced transformers for power quality.
  • Increasing deployment of GIS switchgear for space efficiency.
  • Demand response programs tailored to data center operations.
  • Emergence of microgrids to support data center reliability.
  • Integration of AI for predictive maintenance in power equipment.
