Cross-border transmission investment signals in focus

Signals suggest renewed interest in cross-border transmission projects to unlock generation and improve reliability, with financing and regulatory alignment remaining key execution risks.

Regional planners appear to be prioritizing interconnectors to improve access to diverse generation resources. There is an expectation that multilateral financing and blended finance structures will be central to getting projects moving. Scenario watch includes protracted regulatory alignment and offtake negotiation timelines. EPCs may need to present turnkey packages that include community engagement and local content plans. What to monitor are early-stage feasibility reports, financing term sheets, and regulatory harmonization efforts.

What to watch
  • scan feasibility reports and financing announcements
  • monitor regulatory harmonization initiatives
Action

Prepare modular financing and local-content offers to align with blended-finance preferences for cross-border projects.
