Long-duration storage pilots seen as hedges against seasonal variability

Signals from capacity planning conversations suggest long-duration storage pilots are being prioritized to manage seasonal renewable variability and firm supply for industrial off-takers.

Expectations are that multi-day storage economics will be piloted where renewables dominate annual supply profiles. Scenario watch: developers may pursue hybrid finance models that combine capacity payments with merchant energy sales. What to monitor are pilot procurement terms and performance guarantees tied to seasonal arbitrage. OEMs of flow batteries and thermal storage that can demonstrate degradation curves will be more likely to win early contracts. Grid planners will need to evaluate the value of long-duration assets against transmission upgrades and gas plant retention. Early pilots could validate new revenue streams and influence policy support for longer-duration technologies.

What to watch
  • pilot procurement notices
  • performance guarantee structures
Action

Identify offtakers with seasonal demand profiles and explore pilot partnerships for long-duration solutions.
