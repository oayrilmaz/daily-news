Storage incentives and grid-code adaptation scenario

Expectations are building that policy adjustments to incentivize storage deployment will surface, accompanied by grid-code updates to integrate fast-response assets into system operations.

Policy signals indicate a push to accelerate storage uptake to balance variable generation and provide ancillary services. Regulators appear to be weighing market design changes that better value fast frequency response and synthetic inertia. Scenario watch: new incentive structures could rapidly increase storage procurement demand, pressuring cell and inverter supply chains. Grid-code updates may require firmware and protection adjustments for fast-ramping assets to comply with system needs. There is an expectation of stakeholder debates on market access and settlement for aggregated storage behind the meter. What to monitor are draft rule changes, incentive program specifics, and alignment with interconnection requirements.

What to watch
  • Draft incentive program details
  • Grid-code consultation outcomes
Action

Map project timelines to potential incentive windows and align technical specs with anticipated grid-code changes.
