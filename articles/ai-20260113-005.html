Capacity price volatility remains a scenario risk in winter months

Expectations point to continued volatility in capacity and short-term markets as reserve margins tighten during high-demand winter periods.

Market signals reflect tighter winter reserve expectations in some regions, which can amplify price swings and call options. Scenario watch includes rapid intra-day price movements and stress on balancing services. What to monitor are capacity auction signals and system operator notices about reserve procurement. There is an expectation that intermittent generation variability will keep balancing demand elevated. Traders and asset owners may increase hedging activity to protect margins against sudden price spikes. Longer-term, market design adjustments remain possible to better value fast-response resources. Short-term actions by asset owners could include temporarily changing operational setpoints to capture scarcity revenues.

What to watch
  • reserve procurement announcements
  • intra-day price volatility measures
Action

Review hedging positions and ensure fast-response assets are available to capitalize on scarcity events.
