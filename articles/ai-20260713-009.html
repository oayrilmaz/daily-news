Distributed PV adoption pressures distribution-level planning

Signals from several distribution utilities in LATAM show increased emphasis on hosting capacity studies and flexible tariff designs as rooftop PV adoption accelerates.

Utilities are issuing guidance to clarify interconnection and net-metering rules to manage voltage and protection challenges. The intelligence perspective recommends scenario planning for high DER adoption, including reverse-power flows and protection coordination issues. Planners are exploring dynamic tariffs and locational signals to manage constrained feeders. Scenario watch: sudden uptake following tariff changes could necessitate emergency curtailment rules. What to monitor are updated hosting-capacity maps and tariff pilot announcements.

What to watch
  • Updated hosting-capacity maps and interconnection guidance
  • Tariff pilot launches that incentivize behind-the-meter flexibility
  • Protections and settings changes for reverse-flow scenarios
Action

Request current hosting-capacity data and evaluate revenue models that account for potential curtailment or tariff changes.
